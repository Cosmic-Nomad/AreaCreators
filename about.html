OUR HISTORY

AreaCreators was founded on the 6th of July 2016 by Karalang FK (Saurus26), co-founded by AbsoluteAlpha and I7itI3ull2 to create a collective of content creators that could work together to make better content, learn new skills and having access to various features. Since we started, we have always been growing and improving the quality of our work. We always try our best to make the best content! United we stand as friends – as a family. For fun; for our shared enjoyments; for you. From montages to let’s plays. From parkour to XP: we strive to make the content YOU want to watch, and WE want to make. Gaming is our passion, and we want to see it grow. We want to be there, standing next to it, helping it to improve and develop, as game, as society. Together, anything is possible. We are unity. We are equality. We are community. We are cultivated creativity. We are AreaCreators. Subscribe to our channel to join our journey!
